Sign a document

Sign any file or text with your ML-DSA private key. PQCServer records the signature and timestamp — creating a verifiable certificate anyone can check. Your document never leaves your browser.

How it works: Your browser computes a SHA-256 hash of the document, signs it with your ML-DSA key, and sends only the hash + signature to PQCServer. The document itself is never uploaded. Anyone can verify the certificate using the public key.
